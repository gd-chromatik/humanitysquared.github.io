Why Choose Us?

The current treatment model has serious flaws. Many IOP and SLE providers are operating at the edge of what is legal, and are quite often going well over the line. Enforcement is coming and providers need to prepare for a way to operate with the law. By placing the needs of patient's first, Humanity Squared is perfectly situated to help all involved, the patient, IOP, SLE to navigae the legal and practical issues surrounding the business of behavioral health treatment.
